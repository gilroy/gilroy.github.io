About me

Curiosity killed the cat, but I'm not a cat. Our world is comprised of many systems; whether it be social constructs, political systems, economic behavior, or the array of protocols that make up our modern network infrastructure- everything in life can be boiled down to a system. My goal is to learn, understand, and utilize these systems to make a positive impact on the world around me.


Information Technology is kind of my wheelhouse. I've been tinkering with computers for as long as I can remember, and a lot of subsequent knowledge has come from these experiences. I am currently a Computer Science student at the University of Colorado Boulder. I'm always looking for new opportunities to apply my skills- I encourage you to reach out to me if I can be of use to your project or company. Let's build a brighter future together.
